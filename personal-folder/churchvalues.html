Church Values

General Conference serves as a sacred platform where leaders of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints expound upon and reinforce key gospel principles that lie at the heart of the LDS faith. As members and friends gather, either in person or virtually, they are immersed in teachings that encompass a profound understanding of spiritual truths and guide individuals on their journey of discipleship. Here are some foundational gospel principles often emphasized during General Conference.

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, often known as the LDS Church, stands on a foundation of core values and beliefs that shape the lives of its members. These principles, deeply rooted in scripture and doctrine, provide a moral compass for individuals striving to live in accordance with their faith. As elucidated and reinforced during General Conference, these values encompass a range of spiritual, ethical, and communal ideals.
